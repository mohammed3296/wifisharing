Help Me App

Project Overview

At present, most of our children are likely to be kidnapped. Where criminals kidnap children and exploit these children to bring money by using them to beg or monument or marketing some illegal products such as drugs and others. Unfortunately parents suffer greatly in how to reach their children even if they contact the police. In this project, We We will build an app to allow parents to reach their children. We will split the development of this app in four stages. First, let's talk about stage 1. In this stage you’ll build the core experience of our app.


Stage 1: Uploading the image of the abductor

The user uploads the image of the abducted child, the child's complete data, the abduction method, the location where the hijacking took place, the date of abduction, and the application publishes the image and data to all users. So that if a user sees this child anywhere he or she will inform the parent of the kidnapped child by commenting on the publication or communicating him .


Stage 2: Prevention of abduction

This application allows users to protect themselves from hijacking. The application allows the so-called call button . when the user feels whether a child or a large kid is hijacked he must click on this button and then the application sends a message to the rescue and some of the previous individuals identified after a certain period of time. This message contains the site of the abduction and if the site changes the application updates it When the receivers.


Stage 3: Warning of places where kidnapping is frequent

Our application warns the user when entering an area with frequent abductions to take care and caution. The application identifies those places by making statistics in the most areas where kidnappings have occurred manually and automatically based on complaints submitted by users . This is done using Gis technology .


Stage 4: Photographing and uploading children who appear to be under the hijacking

We see a lot of kids who appear to be kidnapped but do not know what to do . This application allows the user to take the child photo who appears to be abducted and upload his image on the application with a description of where the child was seen. The application then makes a matching for this image with the images which were loaded before and if there is a similarity the app reports the father of the child .
